{{#_}}Guidelines{{/_}}


{{#_}}Anyone can submit ideas.{{/_}}

Eligible ideas must be for capital projects. Projects are eligible for funding if they:

  • {{#_}}Benefit the public. Projects that only benefit private individuals or groups are not eligible.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Are a one-time expenditure that can be completed with funds from this year's PB process ($500,000).{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Are implemented by the City of Cambridge on public property.{{/_}}

{{#_}}Examples of Eligible Capital Ideas{{/_}}


{{#_}}Eligible:{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Street and sidewalk paving{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Community garden{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Park renovations{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Redesigning an intersection to make it safer{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Bike racks and shelters{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Replacing playground equipment{{/_}}
{{#_}}Non-Eligible{{/_}}:

  • {{#_}}A block party of community event{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Running a youth basketball league{{/_}}
